Tired of missing your delivery?

You like shopping online, but you hate ending up at the post office because you missed your delivery? This is just the app you need!

With this app you can request your neighbours to pick up your incoming delivery. You then have to choose the neighbour you like from those who are willing to accept your request.

When you get home you can just get your package from your neighbour instead of having the hassle to reschedule a delivery or go to the post office.

You can also reward them for their service!
